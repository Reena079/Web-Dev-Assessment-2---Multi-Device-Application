Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our rewards program

How do I join Brew & Bean Rewards?

You can join for free by downloading our mobile app, visiting our website, or signing up in-store. No purchase is necessary to join.

How do I earn stars?

Earn 1 star for every $1 spent. Earn bonus stars when you order ahead through our app, pay with a registered gift card, or participate in special promotions.

Do my stars expire?

Stars expire 6 months after they are earned. However, as long as you earn at least 1 star every 6 months, your stars will remain active.

How do I redeem my stars?

You can redeem stars through our mobile app, website, or by telling your barista at any Brew & Bean location. Stars are automatically deducted when you redeem a reward.

Can I share my rewards with friends?

Rewards are non-transferable and can only be redeemed by the account holder. However, you can purchase gift cards for friends and family.

What happens if I lose my phone?

Contact customer service immediately. We can secure your account and help you access it from a new device. Your stars are stored in your account, not on your phone.
