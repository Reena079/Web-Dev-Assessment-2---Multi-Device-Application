Master Your Brew

Pour Over

Clean, bright flavors with precise control over extraction time and temperature.

Key Steps:

  1. Heat water to 200°F (93°C)
  2. Rinse paper filter with hot water
  3. Add coffee (medium grind)
  4. Bloom with water for 30 seconds
  5. Pour remaining water in circles

Perfect for: Light to medium roasts

French Press

Full-bodied, rich coffee with immersion brewing that extracts maximum flavor.

Key Steps:

  1. Coarse grind coffee beans
  2. Add to press with hot water
  3. Steep for 4 minutes
  4. Press plunger slowly
  5. Serve immediately

Perfect for: Dark roasts & blends

Espresso

Concentrated coffee base for specialty drinks with rich crema and bold flavor.

Key Steps:

  1. Fine grind coffee beans
  2. Dose and tamp evenly
  3. Extract at 9 bars pressure
  4. Aim for 25-30 second shot
  5. 36g output from 18g coffee

Perfect for: Specialty drinks
